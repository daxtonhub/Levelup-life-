Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

LevelUp Life ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: email address, username, profile data, journal entries, quest completion data, and usage statistics.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the App's features. Journal entries are private and visible only to you. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data Storage

Data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption. Deleted journal entries are retained for 30 days then permanently removed.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us through the App.

Female Mode Data

Period tracking data is stored privately and is never shared. This data is visible only to you.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us through the App's support section.
